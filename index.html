Bio

I am a first year PhD student in the Robotics program at Oregon State University. I am working with Professor Ross Hatton in the Laboratory for Robotics and Applied Mechanics. I earned my B.S. in Physics from the University of Oregon.

My academic interests include solving non-physics problems with physics math, good documentation, and teaching programming to people of all ages.

In my free time I sew, program little games, and calculate the entropy of Mastermind and Battleship.
